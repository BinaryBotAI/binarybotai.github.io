Request Account and Data Deletion

We understand that you may wish to delete your account and associated data. This page provides instructions on how to submit your request.

How to Request Deletion (Email Method)

Please follow these steps to request the deletion of your account and all associated data via email:

  1. Email Request: Send an email to our dedicated data privacy email address: apptrextechnologies@gmail.com
  2. Subject Line: Use the following subject line in your email: "Account Deletion Request".
  3. Body of Email: In the body of your email, please provide the following information:
    • The email address associated with your account.
    • Your full name (optional, but helpful for verification).
    • Any other information that might help us identify your account (e.g., username, if applicable).
    • A clear statement that you wish to delete your account and all associated data.
  4. Verification: We may contact you to verify your identity before processing your request. This is to protect your account and ensure that the request is legitimate.
  5. Confirmation: Once your account and data have been deleted, we will send you a confirmation email.

Please note that after your account is deleted, your data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered. Some data may be retained for legal or compliance reasons, but it will be anonymized and not linked to your personal identity.

How to Delete Your Account Directly in the App

If you prefer to delete your account directly, you can follow these steps:

Click on the "Profile" icon at the bottom right of the screen).
Select your profile from the menu.
Go to "Account Settings" or a similarly named option.
Click on the "Delete Account" button.

After clicking "Delete Account," your deletion request will be processed.

Note: The direct deletion method only applies if you are using our app
